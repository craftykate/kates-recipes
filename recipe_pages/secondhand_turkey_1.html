Secondhand Turkey

Source: Taste of Home; Rating: 4

Serves: 4; Category: Dinner

Oven Temp: 375; Bake for: 20-25 min

INGREDIENTS:

0.5 lb Mushrooms, fresh, sliced
0.5 cup Celery, chopped
5 tbsp Butter, divided
2 tbsp Cornstarch
2 cup Milk
2 cup Turkey, cubed and cooked
2 cup Egg Noodles, cooked
0.25 cup Chicken Broth
1 tsp Salt
0.5 tsp Thyme, dried
0.125 tsp White Pepper
0.5 cup Bread Crumbs, dry

DIRECTIONS:

In a large skillet, saute the mushrooms and celery in 3 tbsp butter until tender. Combine the cornstarch and milk until smooth; stir into the mushroom mixture. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. Cook for 1 minute or until thickened.

Stir in the turkey, noodles, broth, salt, thyme and pepper. Pour into a greased 2-qt baking dish. Melt remaining butter; toss with bread crumbs. Sprinkle over casserole.

Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees for 20-25 minutes, or until heated through.
