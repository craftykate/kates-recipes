Wasabi Beef Fajitas

Source: Taste of Home

Serves: 8; Category: Dinner

INGREDIENTS:

2 tsp Cornstarch
3 tbsp Soy Sauce
2 tsp Wasabi, prepared
2 tsp Gingerroot, fresh
1 tsp Garlic, minced
1 each Red Pepper, large, julienned
12 each Green Onion, with tops, cut in half lengthwise
2 tbsp Sesame Oil, divided
1 lb Beef Stir-Fry Strips, uncooked
8 each Tortillas, warmed
1 cup Coleslaw Mix

DIRECTIONS:

In a small bowl, combine the cornstarch, soy sauce, wasabi, ginger garlic until blended. Set aside.

In a large skillet, stir-fry red pepper and onions in 1 tbsp oil for 3 minutes or until tender; remove and set aside. In the same skillet, stir-fry beef in remaining oil for 5 minutes or until no longer pink.

Stir the soy sauce mixture; stir into skillet. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Return red pepper mixture ot the pan; heat through.

Spoon 1/2 cup beef mixture down the center of each tortilla; top with 2 tbsp coleslaw mix. Fold one side of tortilla over filling and roll up. Serve immediately.
